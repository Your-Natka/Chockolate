  • COCONUT OIL

    COCONUT OIL

    coconut oil

    the benefits

    Coconut oil is often used in homemade or vegan chocolate because of its texture and flavor benefits. It melts at a temperature of about 24°C, which allows the chocolate to melt in your mouth. It adds a light, pleasant aroma and taste of coconut, which can enhance the chocolate flavor or create an exotic shade.
